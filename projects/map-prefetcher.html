Map Prefetcher

JavaScript Web Performance Mapping

Map Prefetcher is a utility that intelligently prefetches map tiles ahead of where a user is looking — reducing the blank-tile flicker that plagues map-heavy applications when you pan or zoom quickly.

The core idea: by predicting viewport movement and loading adjacent tiles in advance, you can give users a seamless experience that feels fast even on slower connections. A small performance win that makes a noticeable difference in day-to-day use.
