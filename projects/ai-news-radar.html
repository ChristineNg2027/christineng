AI News Radar

AI NLP Ongoing

AI News Radar is an ongoing project to build an AI-powered news aggregator — one that doesn't just collect headlines, but actually surfaces what's worth paying attention to.

The idea is to cut through the noise: using language models to cluster, rank, and contextualize stories so you spend less time scrolling and more time understanding. Still actively building this one.
