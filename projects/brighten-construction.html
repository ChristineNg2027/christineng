BrightenConstruction

Web App Firebase Local Business

BrightenConstruction is a platform built to empower local construction businesses — giving them a digital presence they might not otherwise have the resources to build themselves.

The goal was to lower the barrier for small contractors and builders to showcase their work, reach new clients, and compete in a market increasingly shaped by online visibility. Sometimes the most meaningful projects are the ones that help people who are quietly building the world around us.
