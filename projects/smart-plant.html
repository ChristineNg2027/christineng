Smart Plant

AI Hardware IoT

Plants can't talk — but they're always communicating. Smart Plant uses AI to interpret a plant's physical signals and infer its current "emotional state": whether it's thriving, stressed, thirsty, or just right.

Based on that reading, the system recommends the right care at the right time — no more guessing whether to water, adjust the light, or just leave it alone. The next step is an LCD display that shows the plant's emotion directly, giving it a little personality of its own.

It started as a curiosity: what if we could actually listen to plants? This project is an attempt to find out.
